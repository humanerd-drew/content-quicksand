A reader finds your post. Reads it. Closes the tab. Sound familiar?

Most plugins solve one piece — related posts OR series nav OR in-content links. You need all three, working together, with zero setup.

3

Bloated suites (Jetpack, etc.) bundle 50+ modules you don't need. Single-feature plugins leave gaps. Content Quicksand plugs all three holes at once.

Three zones. One post. Zero configuration.

All three retention layers activate automatically on every new post. For existing content, use the admin dashboard or WP-CLI to generate links in bulk.

Series Navigation auto-detects Ep.1, Part 2 patterns — rendered on every series post
Readers see where they are and jump to the next (or previous) part. Works with Ep.1, Part 2, 에피소드 3 patterns.
In-Content Links contextual inline links injected based on keyword matching
TF-IDF + keyword matching + taxonomy scoring. Links appear inline in the post body. No LLM needed.
Related Posts Grid 6 taxonomy-matched cards below every post
Multi-dimensional scoring — series, category, recency, and tags. Up to 6 cards per post. Pure PHP, no external APIs.
