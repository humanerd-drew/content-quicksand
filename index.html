The Leaky Bucket

A reader finds your post. Reads it. Closes the tab. Sound familiar?

70–85%

of first-time visitors never return to a typical WordPress site. Your content is good — but your site has no mechanism to keep readers moving.

The real problem isn't your content.

It's that your site is missing three things: a way to show readers where they are in a series, links that pull them deeper mid-post, and a grid of next reads when they finish. Most plugins solve one of these. You need all three, working together, with zero setup.

Bloated suites are overkill.

Jetpack and friends bundle 50+ modules when you just want readers to find the next post. Single-feature plugins leave gaps — your series nav works but there's no related posts grid.

Four features. One plugin. Zero setup.

Series Navigation

Automatically detects post series (Ep.1, Part 2, 에피소드 3) and renders a progress bar with prev/next links. Readers always know where they are.

Keeps series readers moving forward

Related Posts Grid

A responsive grid appears below every post with thumbnails and titles of related content. Taxonomy-matched, not random. Up to 6 cards.

Catches readers before they bounce

In-Content Links

Contextual links injected mid-post based on shared categories or tags. Subtle, not intrusive. Styled to match your theme. No LLM, no Python.

Pulls readers deeper into your archive

Zero Setup

No settings page. No API keys. No cron jobs. Activate the plugin and all three features work immediately. Pure PHP, no external dependencies.
